All Hemodynamic Phenotyping

HemoPheno4HF is an open source risk stratification and phenotyping tool for hemodynamic phenotypes in advanced heart failure. This tool uses a variety of medical parameters to provide a hemodynamic risk score of 1 to 5, indicating the probability of a specified outcome. For example, a risk score of 5 (high) may indicate the patient has a greater than 40% probability of the specified outcome, whereas a score of 1 (low) may indicate a less than 10% probability of the outcome.

Please enter the wanted parameters below, and select the appropriate outcome you wish to generate a score for. You may also auto-fill example parameters using the provided buttons. Any terms can be hovered over to show the acronym spelled out, should there be confusion or uncertainty.

More information: https://github.com/jozieLamp/HemoPheno4HF
